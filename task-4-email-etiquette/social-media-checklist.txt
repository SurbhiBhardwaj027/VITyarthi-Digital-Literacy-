Responsible Social Media Use Checklist (For College Students)

DO’S
• Think before you post – Make sure your content is respectful and appropriate.
• Protect your privacy – Adjust privacy settings and avoid sharing personal details (address, phone number, etc.).
• Verify information – Check facts before sharing news or posts to avoid spreading misinformation.
• Be respectful online – Engage in positive and constructive conversations.
• Manage your time – Set limits to avoid excessive use and stay focused on studies.

Don’ts
• Don’t share sensitive information – Avoid posting passwords, financial details, or private data.
• Don’t engage in cyberbullying – Never harass, troll, or insult others online.
• Don’t believe everything you see – Not all online content is true or reliable.
• Don’t overshare personal life – Too much sharing can harm your reputation and safety.
• Don’t use social media during important tasks – It can reduce productivity and concentration.
